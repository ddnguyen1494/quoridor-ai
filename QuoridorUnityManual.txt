Build executable is in folder
How to play:
Left-Click on square you wish to move to
To place a Horizontal wall, Left-click on green peg where you wish to place it
To place a Vertical wall, Right-click on a green peg where you wish to place it
At any point you may click the ESC-key to quit the application
